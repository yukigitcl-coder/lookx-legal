Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

LOOKX ("the App") handles your personal information and photo data with care. This policy describes what we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and your rights.

1. Operator

Service: LOOKX
Operator: LOOKX Team
Contact: support@lookx.app

2. Information we collect

  • Account info: email address, display name, avatar (optional)
  • Face photos: photos you upload for AI analysis
  • Diagnosis results: scores, advice, and recommendations generated by AI
  • Billing info: purchase history and subscription status (via payment processors)
  • Usage logs: app usage and error logs (no personally identifying details)

3. Handling of face photos

Face photos are treated as sensitive information. We follow these rules:
  • Uploaded photos are sent over HTTPS to external AI APIs (Anthropic Claude / Google Gemini) for analysis.
  • Photos are not saved by default. Temporary data is discarded after analysis.
  • Photos are stored on Supabase Storage only if you explicitly choose to save, and only linked to your account.
  • We do not perform facial identification or attempt to identify individuals.
  • We never sell or share your photos with third parties.
  • We do not use your photos to train AI models (API calls remain within the providers' standard usage).

4. How we use your data

  • Generate and deliver AI-based appearance analysis
  • Manage your saved history (if you opt in)
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Improve the service and fix bugs
  • Prevent abuse and fraud

5. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services, limited to what is necessary to provide the service.

  • Anthropic (Claude API): AI analysis
  • Google (Gemini API): AI analysis for free-trial
  • Supabase: authentication, database, storage
  • Stripe: web payments
  • RevenueCat: mobile in-app purchases
  • Google AdMob: mobile ads
  • Google Ad Manager: web ads

6. Retention

  • Photos and results you saved: until you delete them or close your account
  • Photos you did NOT save: discarded immediately after analysis
  • Account info: until you delete the account
  • Purchase records: up to 7 years (legal requirements)

7. Your rights

At any time you may:

  • View or edit your data
  • Delete saved photos and results
  • Delete your entire account
  • Contact us regarding data handling (subject to identity verification)

Send deletion requests to support@lookx.app.

8. Minors

The App is intended for users aged 13 and older. Users under 13 may not use the App without parental consent.

9. Security

We encrypt all traffic with HTTPS and restrict data access via account authentication. We cannot, however, guarantee absolute security of internet transmissions or storage.

10. About AI output

Diagnosis results are generated by AI and are not medical advice. If you have concerns (e.g. about your skin), please consult a qualified professional such as a dermatologist.

11. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated without prior notice. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notification or email.

12. Contact

For questions about this policy, contact support@lookx.app.
